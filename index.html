Hear From the Founder 

At Bloomz Fertility, every journey begins with hope — and my purpose is to help women rediscover it.

I’m Dr. Saliha Shabnam, Homeopathic Physician with a BHMS graduation from Government Homoeopathic Medical College, Calicut, and a Master’s in Clinical Nutrition & Dietetics from St. Teresa’s College.

With over 10 years of dedicated experience in women’s health, I have had the privilege of supporting women through some of the most sensitive and transformative phases of life. This journey led to the creation of Bloomz Fertility — the fertility care department of Arha Homoeopathy — envisioned as a compassionate, science-backed space for women seeking natural and holistic healing.
At Bloomz, we focus on female health concerns including PCOS, endometriosis, adenomyosis, irregular menstrual bleeding, thyroid, dysmenorrhea, hormonal issues, infertility etc.. addressing root causes rather than suppressing symptoms.
By blending the gentle wisdom of Homeopathy with the precision of clinical reproductive nutrition, our approach supports hormonal balance, cycle regulation, and optimal fertility — treating the woman as a whole, not just her diagnosis.
The name Bloomz reflects a belief close to my heart: every woman deserves the opportunity to bloom into her healthiest self — and to bring a healthy new bloom into this world.
My mission remains simple yet profound — to guide women from uncertainty to clarity, from struggle to strength, and from hope to motherhood — one step at a time.
